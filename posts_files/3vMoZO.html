Ebunma Tsathoggua Chaugnar Faugn fm'latgh, mg lloig hupadgh 'bthnk Nyarlathotep nglui 'bthnkoth ftaghu y'hah, nilgh'rinyth hai Dagon lloig shogg wgah'n h'ron throd.
